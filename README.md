## SIDISH
SIDISH is a novel deep learning framework that integrates the high resolution of single-cell RNA sequencing with the broad clinical insights of bulk RNA sequencing. By combining Variational Autoencoder (VAE), deep Cox regression, and transfer learning, SIDISH dynamically refines predictions through iterative cycles, identifying clinically significant high-risk cells associated with poor survival outcomes. One of its key features is in-silico gene perturbation, enabling the simulation of gene knockouts to predict their effects on high-risk cells, helping to uncover potential therapeutic targets.

SIDISH offers a robust and scalable approach for advancing precision medicine, improving both biomarker discovery and therapeutic target identification.
